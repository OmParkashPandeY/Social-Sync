About Us

Social Sync is a one-stop social media management platform that helps you create, publish, and analyze content across all of your social media channels. We offer a variety of features to help you save time and improve your social media results, including:

Post scheduler: Schedule posts to be published on all of your social media channels at the best time to reach your audience.

Content calendar: Plan your social media content in advance and see how it will look across all of your channels.

Analytics: Track your social media performance and identify areas for improvement.

Free image resources: Access a library of over 1 million free images to use in your social media posts.

Image editor tools: Edit your images before you publish them to make them look their best.

Download: Download your posts as images or videos to share on other platforms.
